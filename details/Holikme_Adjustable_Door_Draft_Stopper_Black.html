Holikme Adjustable Door Draft Stopper

Keep your home warm and cozy with the Holikme Adjustable Door Draft Stopper. Designed to create a tight seal against drafts, it effectively blocks cold air, heat, smoke, and light from entering or escaping through your door. This versatile product is ideal for all types of doors and offers a perfect solution for maintaining indoor comfort throughout the year.

Featuring a durable, high-quality sponge construction, the stopper resists shifting with door movement and stays firmly in place for consistent insulation. The hook and loop fastening system ensures a secure fit, while the detachable design makes cleaning and repositioning effortless. Plus, it's machine washable for easy maintenance and safe for reuse without damaging your door.
